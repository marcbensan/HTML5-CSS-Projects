About Tarantulas

Welcome to the Tarantula Educational Website! This website is created by Marc Bensan. This website is your opportunity to wonders of these creatures! Tarantulas are heavily misunderstood species due to the media's portrayal. In this educational website, The goal of this website is to change people's mindset towards these amazing specimen. As you explore this site, you'll find yourself captivated by these misunderstood creatures, realizing that they are an essential part of our natural world, deserving of respect and understanding.
